Mobile app only

Built for the moment between saving a link and cooking from it.

Recipe sites are built to keep you scrolling. LinkDish is built for the quieter part: getting the ingredients, steps, timing, and notes onto your phone so you can actually cook without popups, autoplay, or hunting through paragraphs again.

Workflow

From recipe link to cooking view.

1

Paste a recipe URL

Start with a public recipe page, article, or supported YouTube recipe link from your phone.

2

Review the clean result

LinkDish extracts the title, ingredients, method, servings, timing, and available nutrition details.

3

Save what you use

Keep useful recipes locally on your device so the next cooking session starts faster.

Straightforward by design

No account is required for one-device recipe imports and saves.

Sign in is only needed for LinkDish Family household sharing. Saved recipes stay on your device, and subscriptions are managed through the app stores when paid access is used.

  • Local saved recipes for normal one-device use.
  • Optional Family household sign-in when sharing is enabled.
  • No third-party advertising in the current app.
