Last updated: June 17, 2026

Help for recipe links, saved recipes, and accounts

Choose the support path that fits what you need.

LinkDish is designed to keep recipe extraction simple and dependable. The options below help route recipe issues, account questions, privacy requests, and web app problems with the details needed to investigate them.

Email Support Open Web App
1 Recipe import help Send the recipe link, what LinkDish returned, and what looked wrong or incomplete. 2 Account and household help Get help with sign in, LinkDish Family households, sharing, or account deletion access. 3 Web app troubleshooting Include your browser, device, and the page or action that failed in the web app. 4 Privacy and data requests Ask about privacy, request account deletion help, or report a data concern.

What to include

The most useful support emails include the recipe link or account area involved, the device and browser or app version, what you expected, and what happened instead.

  • Recipe URL or LinkDish page where the problem happened.
  • Device, browser, Android app version, or iOS web app details.
  • Any error message, screenshot, or short screen recording.
  • Whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes.

Fast first checks

If something is not working as expected, these quick checks often solve the issue or make the support request easier to trace:

  • Make sure the recipe link is public and still available.
  • Try pasting the link again if the source was temporarily unreachable.
  • Try a different recipe source if the site blocks extraction or is unsupported.

Saved recipes

Saved recipes are stored on your device. If a saved recipe looks outdated or malformed after an app update, removing it and extracting it again should refresh the content.

Accounts and Family households

No account is required to import and save recipes on one device. Sign in is required only to create, join, or manage LinkDish Family household sharing.

Privacy

For details about what information the app handles, read the LinkDish Privacy Policy.

Contact

For support, feedback, or privacy questions, email robertgordon761@gmail.com. If you are using the web app, include the browser and device you are using so the issue is easier to trace.

Data deletion requests

If you created a LinkDish account, you can delete it from Account in the app. Deleting the account removes server-side account and household records, but paid subscriptions still need to be managed through the checkout or store account used to subscribe. If you cannot access the app, send your request to robertgordon761@gmail.com.
