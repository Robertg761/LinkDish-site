Support

Last updated: May 11, 2026

LinkDish is designed to keep recipe extraction simple and dependable. If something is not working as expected, the fastest first checks are:

  • Make sure the recipe link is public and still available.
  • Try pasting the link again if the source was temporarily unreachable.
  • Try a different recipe source if the site blocks extraction or is unsupported.

Saved recipes

Saving recipes requires LinkDish Plus or Family. Personal saved recipes are stored on your device. If a saved recipe looks outdated or malformed after an app update, removing it and extracting it again should refresh the content.

Accounts and Family households

No account is required for the first 3 free recipe imports. Sign in is required to buy or restore a paid plan, create, join, or manage LinkDish Family household sharing, or add recipes to a shared Family recipe book.

Privacy

For details about what information the app handles, read the LinkDish Privacy Policy.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, email support@linkdish.ca. If you are testing a pre-release build, you can also use the same channel where you received the build to report issues.

Data deletion requests

If you created a LinkDish account, you can delete it from Account in the app. Deleting the account removes server-side account and household records, but App Store or Google Play subscriptions still need to be managed in the store. If you cannot access the app, send your request to support@linkdish.ca.
