Good to know

Answers before you send.

Why did a recipe import look incomplete?

Some recipe sites publish ingredients, steps, timing, or notes in formats that are difficult to read automatically. Send the original link and tell us what looked wrong so the source can be checked.

Where are personal saved recipes stored?

Free includes up to 15 personal saved recipes, and paid plans include unlimited saves. Personal recipes are stored on your device. If one looks outdated or malformed after an update, remove it and import it again.

When do I need an account?

No account is required for the first 3 free recipe imports. Sign in is required to buy or restore a plan and to create, join, or manage a LinkDish Family household.

How do I delete my account?

Delete it from Account in the app. If you cannot access the app, submit a Privacy or data request using the email address connected to the account. Store subscriptions must still be managed through the App Store or Google Play, and web subscriptions through the billing portal opened from LinkDish.

Where can I read the privacy policy?

Read the complete LinkDish Privacy Policy for details about accounts, analytics, purchases, recipes, and data requests.
